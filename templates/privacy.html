Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Introduction

Fellowship With The Word ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect information when you use our app and website at fellowshipwiththeword.org.

Information We Collect

When you create an account we collect your name and email address. Your password is never stored in plain text — it is securely hashed using industry standard encryption before being saved.

If you save devotionals or sermons as favourites, we store a record linking your account to those items so you can access them later.

You can use most of Fellowship With The Word, including reading devotionals, sermons and the Bible, without creating an account.

How We Use Your Information

We use your name and email to identify your account and allow you to log in. We use your saved favourites only to display them back to you on your profile.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third Party Services

Our app uses the following third party services:

Bible API (bible-api.com) — Used to retrieve Bible verses. When you search for a verse your query is sent to this service.

YouTube — Our sermons page embeds YouTube videos. YouTube may collect data when you watch these videos. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details.

Render — Our app is hosted on Render. Please refer to Render's privacy policy for details on their data handling.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Passwords are hashed and never stored in readable form. Our database is hosted on secure infrastructure.

Cookies and Sessions

Our app uses session cookies to keep you logged in. These cookies contain no readable personal information and expire after 30 days or when you log out.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time from within the app. Go to Profile and tap "Delete my account". You can also contact us at fwtword@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Fellowship With The Word is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

As Fellowship With The Word grows we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly. We will always display the date of the last update at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact us at fwtword@gmail.com or through our Contact page.
