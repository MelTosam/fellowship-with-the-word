Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

Fellowship With The Word ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app and website at fellowshipwiththeword.org.

Information We Collect

Currently Fellowship With The Word does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to access our content.

We do not collect, store or share any personal data from our users at this time.

Third Party Services

Our app uses the following third party services:

Bible API (bible-api.com) — Used to retrieve Bible verses. When you search for a verse your query is sent to this service. Please refer to their privacy policy for details.

YouTube — Our sermons page embeds YouTube videos. YouTube may collect data when you watch these videos. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details.

Render — Our app is hosted on Render. Please refer to Render's privacy policy for details on their data handling.

Cookies

Our app uses a session cookie solely to keep you logged in to the admin area if you are an administrator. This cookie contains no personal information and is deleted when you close your browser.

Children's Privacy

Fellowship With The Word is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

As Fellowship With The Word grows and adds new features such as user accounts, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly. We will always display the date of the last update at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy please contact us through our Contact page.
