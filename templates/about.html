About Us

Fellowship With The Word

Networking the world with the light of life — John 1:4

Our Vision

Fellowship With The Word is a global ministry dedicated to spreading the light of God's Word to every nation, city and community. We believe the Word of God is alive, powerful and transformative — and that every person deserves access to it.

Our Mission

To network the world with the light of life through daily devotionals, Bible teaching and sermon content — reaching people across Africa, Europe, the Americas, Asia and beyond with the unchanging truth of Scripture.

What We Offer

Daily devotionals to feed your spirit every morning. Sermon teachings rooted in the Word. A full Bible reader to help you study and grow. All freely available to anyone, anywhere in the world.

Our Foundation

"In him was life, and that life was the light of all mankind."

John 1:4
