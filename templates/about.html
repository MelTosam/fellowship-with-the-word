About Us

Our Vision

Networking the world with the light of life — John 1:4

Our Mission

Fellowship With The Word exists to reveal the love of God in Christ Jesus and renew the minds of believers to the accurate knowledge of God. We believe the Bible is a Christocentric material — every scripture points to Christ.

What We Believe

We believe in the inherent integrity of the Word of God to work in the lives of men. Our focus is on revealing God as a God of love — contrary to the doctrines being taught in many churches today.

We believe true transformation only happens in the life of a believer when the accurate word of God is taught. This involves proper Bible interpretation paying attention to context, language and cultural context. Freedom in Christ starts with the accurate knowledge of the character of God.

Our Community

We started as a Bible study group where we gathered from different parts of the world to study and grow in the knowledge of the Word of God. As we have grown over time, we want to carry this knowledge and equip the body of Christ for the work of ministry.

Everyone who hungers for the accurate knowledge of God is welcome here.
